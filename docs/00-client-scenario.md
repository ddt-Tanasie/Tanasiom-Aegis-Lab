# Client Scenario

## Client
Northbridge Logistics Ltd

## Company Profile
Northbridge Logistics is a small logistics and distribution company with approximately 25 employees.

The company relies on a small internal IT infrastructure consisting of workstations, a web server, and legacy internal services.

## Engagement Objective

The client has engaged Tanasiom Aegis to perform an internal infrastructure security review in preparation for improving its cybersecurity posture and working towards Cyber Essentials readiness.

## Assessment Scope

The following systems are included within scope:

- Windows 10 employee workstation
- Ubuntu Server hosting internal web services
- Legacy Metasploitable system representing outdated infrastructure

## Network

Internal subnet:

192.168.56.0/24

## Objective

Identify exposed services, insecure configurations, and potential attack paths that could allow unauthorised access to internal systems.
